Connect With Other Artists

Looking to connect with other artists who share the same passion for music as you? Your search is over. The STAR FACTOR community is THE number one online forum for budding and accomplished musicians alike. Connect with your favorite professional musicians, or find the missing member of your band within your local community. Below are just a few of our accomplished contributors active within our community. What are you waiting for? Get chatting, and Shine Up, to Shine On!

Randy Blythe

Lamb Of God

An American singer, best known as the vocalist and lyricist of American heavy metal band Lamb of God. He has also performed guest vocals for Cannabis Corpse, Overkill, Gojira, and EYEHATEGOD and he is also the lead singer of side-project band Halo of Locusts.

Phil Anselmo

Pantera

An American heavy metal musician who is best known as the lead vocalist for Pantera. He is also the owner of Housecore Records and has been involved with several other bands.

Corey Taylor

Slipknot, Stone Sour

An American musician, singer, songwriter, actor, and author, best known as the lead singer and lyricist of the American heavy metal band Slipknot and the American alternative metal band Stone Sour.

Kerry King

Slayer

An American musician, best known as a rhythm guitarist for the American thrash metal band Slayer. He co-founded the band with Jeff Hanneman in 1981 and has been a member ever since. He became lead songwriter for the band after Hanneman's death.
